    Survey Test Results
Buddies, Not Bullies.
Our workshop is entitled, “Buddies, not Bullies”.
Our workshop occurred in Google Meet last April 14, 2021.
We had an introduction and the purpose of the workshop.
We had let them watch videos, answer questions, and a quiz.
Then after that we tackled the three sessions we had. After that, we had a Q&A.
And lastly, we gave them a short survey about our workshop.
The purpose of our usability test is to know what they liked the most about our workshop,
if our workshop is effective, and lastly what our workshop can improve on.
We created a quiz for our workshop. The content of our quiz is about our topic
which is cyberbullying.
We asked our participants if they are aware of cyberbullying,
if they bullied someone before,
what they felt about our workshop, and etc.
After collecting the data from our form, 15 students from Grade 9PM- 4
from APEC Roxas and Las Piñas answered it.
All our participants are from 9PM-4. Participants #1, #5, #7, #8, are 14 year-old females.
Participants #3, #6, #10, #12, #13, #14 are 15 year-old females.
Participants #2, and #4 are 14 year-old males.
Lastly, participants #9, #11, and #15 are 15 year-old males.
Our workshop lasted for less than an hour. We didn’t experience a major problem.
Participants are now well aware of the effects of cyberbullying on an individual.
But, we were able to deliver our workshop properly.
Our workshop made another survey for the participants.
Most of our participants had a good experience in joining our workshop.
They felt excited, and informed because they learned a lot of information.
Our workshop was helpful because we gave an informative presentation.
Many have learned from our workshop, what cyberbullying is and the effects of it.
If they were to rate our workshop from 1-5, 1 as the lowest and 5 as the highest,
80% answered 5 while 20% answered 4.
Lastly, things that we can improve on our workshop is the slides and how we presented
(most of us was nervous and had technical difficulties).
